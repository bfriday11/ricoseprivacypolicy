Data Retention & Deletion Policy

Warning: Account deletion is absolute. Once confirmed, your in-game progress, high scores, customization records, and profile details cannot be recovered.

Types of Data Deleted

Upon clicking the final deletion sequence, the following variables are completely purged from our structural servers:

  • Authentication Profile: Login credentials, email paths, and unique matching database User IDs (`UUID`).
  • Personal Data: Display names, profile handles, and custom game settings.
  • Application Data: High scores, localized save states, game progression stats, and activity logs.

Data We Are Required to Keep (Retention Period)

All identifying data structures are completely zeroed out except for records legally or operationally required for preservation:

  • Financial & Purchase History: In-app transaction details handled via Google Play are archived safely for 7 years to satisfy regulatory tax documentation obligations and auditing frameworks.
  • Anonymized Metrics: Purely functional, aggregated behavioral metrics may stay within telemetry trackers (this structured tracking is totally stripped of unique identifiers).
